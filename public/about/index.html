About Interact Tape ConverterGo Back
  • This tool is designed for reading and writing Interact or Hector cassettes in .wav, .k7, or .cin format so that you can either create archives or use these tapes with emulators or real Interact or Hector machines.
  • Tapes can be loaded from any audio format but 44khz mono is recommended when digitizing real tapes.
  • .K7 and .CIN files are used by many Interact and Hector emulators such as MAME/MESS, DCHector, and Virtual Interact.
